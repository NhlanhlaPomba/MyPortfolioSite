✓ Completed - November 2025

Developed an AI-powered diagnostic tool for real-time respiratory disease classification using embedded systems and deep learning. Hybrid architecture combines ESP32 audio acquisition with desktop PC inference, achieving 90.14% accuracy across 6 respiratory conditions at 96.5% cost reduction compared to commercial alternatives.

Key Achievements:

  • Achieved 90.14% classification accuracy on ICBHI 2017 dataset (Bronchiectasis, Bronchiolitis, COPD, Healthy, Pneumonia, URTI)

  • Developed multi-channel feature extraction: 128-mel spectrograms, 13 MFCCs, 12 chroma (153×259 total)
  • Built deep CNN with SeparableConv2D, BatchNormalization, and focal loss for class imbalance
  • Total system cost: R295 (96.5% cheaper than commercial AI stethoscopes at R8,500+)

Technologies Used:

ESP32-WROOM-32MAX4466 MicrophonePython TensorFlow/KerasTensorFlow LiteLibrosa SMOTEFocal LossTkinter GUI
